NEVER use these words in any copy or AI agent responses:
- "Promise" — creates legally binding obligation
- "Guarantee results" — CROA/FTC violation
- "We will" as contractual language
- "You will make money" / "You will earn" — SEC/FTC income claims
- "Results guaranteed" — direct CROA violation

ALWAYS use instead:
- "Our system is designed to" / "built to deliver"
- "Members who follow the system typically"
- "Positions you for" instead of "you will get"
- "$12K/month is the goal the system is built around" (not guaranteed income)
